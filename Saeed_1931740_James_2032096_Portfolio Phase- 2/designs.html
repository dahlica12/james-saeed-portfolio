Liked:


  • We like how the dark background contrasts to the light text; it makes the page easily readible
  • Images appear when you hover your cursor over the main screen, a visually pleasing and unique affect
  • The colors change as you scroll down the site; making it easy to differentiate the topics
  • The text is organized with a nice readible font



  • The images are relevant and help you visualize the content of the text
  • The white font color compliments the darker background colors
  • Each section has it's own color and the geometric shapes give it a good touch


Disliked:


  • Colors do not constrast with each other; making it difficult to look at
  • Not organized in a neat and orderly fashion
  • Background-colors of page headings do not match the color stripe that runs down the webpage (very unprofessional and messy)
